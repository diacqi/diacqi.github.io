Pride, Our Undoing

Mon Jun 14, 2021
poem

for the world to decay,
we would need to take much away.
humanity’s pride would do the deed
humanity’s extinction would be guaranteed.

in a planet of 8 million kinds
of living beings
it is a folly to believe that
we matter the most.
                                ~ Nikhil
